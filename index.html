About Us

With years of experience in woodworking,in a small workshop in the back fo his house, there is just one person behind every piece, Nonno, a humble carpenter. From a young age, he has poured his heart into every handcrafted wooden piece. With each stroke of his chisel, he expresses a deep love for craftsmanship. His passion isn’t just in the wood—it’s in the stories he creates, the beauty he shares, and the legacy he hopes to pass on. Every piece we craft is a piece of his soul, and it is with this love that we bring unique furniture into your home.
